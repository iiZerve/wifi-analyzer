Privacy Policy for WiFi Analyzer

Last updated: June 6, 2026

WiFi Analyzer (“the App”) is developed by Zerve. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App performs WiFi network scans using your device’s WiFi hardware and the Android WiFiManager APIs. This requires the ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION permission because modern versions of Android mandate it to return WiFi scan results and current connection information.

Information collected locally includes:

  • Nearby WiFi network SSIDs, BSSIDs (MAC addresses), signal strength (dBm), frequency, channel, channel width, and security capabilities.
  • Information about your currently connected network (SSID, BSSID, signal strength, link speed, IP address) when available.
  • Accelerometer / motion data (during Walking Survey mode only) to detect movement between scans.

No data is sent over the network by the App itself.

How We Use the Information

All scan results, analysis, rogue detection, Walking Survey data, guidance, and reports are processed entirely on your device. This enables the core features:

  • Real-time WiFi scanning and health scoring
  • Walking Survey with movement tracking and real-time guidance
  • Rogue / suspicious open network detection
  • Path Optimizer recommendations
  • Export of your own data

Data Sharing and Retention

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your WiFi scan data, location, or personal information with Zerve or any third parties.

The only way data leaves your device is if you explicitly choose to export it (as a JSON file that you control).

The App does not retain data after you close it, except for data you have exported or saved yourself.

Permissions Used

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — Required by Android to obtain accurate WiFi scan results and current connection details.
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE and CHANGE_WIFI_STATE — Used to perform WiFi scans.

We do not use these permissions for any other purpose (e.g. no background tracking or advertising).

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
