## Full Description (up to 4000 characters)

WiFi Analyzer delivers real scans directly from your device’s WiFi hardware. Every network, signal reading, channel, and rogue detection uses actual data — no mocks, no random numbers, no simulations.

Key capabilities:
• Live WiFi scan with signal strength, band (2.4/5/6 GHz), channel, channel width, and security type (WPA3, WPA2, Open, etc.)
• Automatic detection of your currently connected network, even on Android 10 and newer
• “Last scan” freshness indicator showing exactly how recent the data is (FRESH / RECENT / STALE)
• Walking Survey mode: repeated real scans while you move through a space, with built-in accelerometer-based movement detection and live guidance (“Signal improving – keep walking”, “High congestion detected”, rogue alerts, etc.)
• Intelligent rogue and suspicious open network detection with High / Medium / Low risk scoring and evil-twin heuristics
• End-of-survey Path Optimizer summary with best observed location by signal strength, average signal across samples, movement stats, and practical router or mesh placement recommendations
• One-tap rich export of complete scan data, full survey history, rogues, and guidance

The app requests location permission only because modern Android requires it to return accurate WiFi scan results and current connection information. All analysis and storage happens locally on your device.

Use WiFi Analyzer to:
- Diagnose weak signal areas in your home or office
- Choose better WiFi channels
- Evaluate router, access point, or mesh system placement while walking the space
- Identify potentially risky open networks
- Understand congestion and band usage in real time

Privacy: The app never sends scan data, location information, or any personal data to external servers. Your results stay on your device unless you explicitly export them as a file you control.

All scanning, survey, and analysis functionality uses only real data collected from your device at the time of use.
